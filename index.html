What is this?

This is a modern twist on the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game. Choose your mode, challenge a friend or test your skills against AI!

×

🧠 How to Play

  • The game is played on a 3×3 grid.
  • Two players take turns — one as "X", the other as "O".
  • You can only have 3 active moves on the board at a time.
  • If you place a fourth move, your oldest move disappears.
  • The oldest move will also appear faded, so you know it's about to go!
  • You win if your current 3 marks line up horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • If the board is full and no one wins, it's a tie. 🤝
  • Click the reset button any time to start over!
